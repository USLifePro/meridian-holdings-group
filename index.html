Meridian Holdings Group

Turning the cash value in life insurance policies into a valuable investment opportunity.

World-class asset backing, liquidity, and safety at an attractive price

At Meridian Holdings Group, we acquire life insurance policies issued by some of the oldest, most finically-stable insurance carriers in America. Given our relationships with a national network of agents and brokers, we are able to source large volumes of policies. These carriers are amongst the highest-rated companies by one or more Nationally Recognized Statistical Rating Organizations (NRSROs).

We secure the cash value embedded in these policies and provide loans against their value to policyholders. We then pool these loans into a securitization to provide investors with a high-quality, highly-rated investment vehicle with an attractive relative-value return.

Our Advantages

High Liquidity

Immediate access to asset values ensures investors can be repaid at any time.

Safety & Security

100% principal and interest backed, with strong guarantees from top-rated carriers.

Attractive Returns

Competitive yields that outperform equivalent market offerings.

Guaranteed Interest

Reliable income stream supported by the cash values of life insurance policies.

Pristine Collateral

Only the strongest-performing policies from highly rated insurers are included in the portfolio.

Smart Structure

Investor notes are protected by a first lien and subordinated equity, creating layered security and transparency.
